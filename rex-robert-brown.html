    Rex Career

    Brown joined Pantera on bass in 1982 replacing Tommy D. Bradford. He was credited as “Rex Rocker” on Pantera’s first four albums: Metal Magic, Projects in the Jungle, I Am the Night and Power Metal. It was announced in 2022 that he would be joining Anselmo, along with Zakk Wylde and Charlie Benante, for Pantera’s first world tour in 2023.
