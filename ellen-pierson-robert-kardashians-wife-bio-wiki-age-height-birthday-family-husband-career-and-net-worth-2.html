    Ellen Pierson Career

    Ellen began her long profession in the real estate industry in the 1970s. During the time, she was running her own real estate firm, J.M Pearson Real Estate, which was located in the Santa Clara area of California.

    Ellen was the company’s vice-president till it was closed in 1984. A year later, she began to work as a Title Insurance sales representative till 2001. She was the director of sales at Heritage Escrow, a firm based in Palm Desert from 2000 – 2008.

    More recently in February 2019, Ellen became the corporate sales director at Westminister Title Company Inc. in the Greater San Diego Area. Her profession has traversed decades. Even though her name might not be one of the most celebrated in real estate, her hard work has earned her numerous prestigious awards.

    At the Willis Allen Wounded Warriors Golf Tournament in August 2015, Ellen received the Longest Drive Award. She is also known to be a three-time winner of the Affiliate of the Year award. She has also won many other awards for her sales performance in the Title Insurance Industry.
