Is Tanjiro's father a demon?

At the start of the first episode of the first season of Demon Slayer, we saw Tanjiro Kamado find his mother and siblings killed by a demon. What's even worse, one of his sisters, Nezuko Kamado, began to turn into a demon. What we didn't see, however, was his father.

Is Yoriichi Tanjiro father?

No, Yoriichi is not directly related to Tanjiro or the Kamado family. The physical similarities are a coincidence, while the earrings and the Hinokami Kagura are Tanjiro's family heirloom, given and taught to him thanks to Yoriichi being close friends with Tanjiro's distant relative, Sumiyoshi.

Does Tanjiro become a demon?

In the far chapters of the Demon Slayer manga, Tanjiro is actually converted into a demon by Muzan Kibutsuji. Muzan does this to continue his demon legacy and live in this world even after being dead.
