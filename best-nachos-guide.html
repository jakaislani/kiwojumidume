Best Nachos: The Best Tortilla Chips, Toppings, &amp; More

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Nachos are like snowflakes—they’re beautiful and no two are alike. The best nachos might be topped with shredded cheese or they might be drenched in a melty cheese sauce. They might involve dairy or they might be totally plant-based. They might have a burst of brine from a scattering of sliced black olives—or if olives make you gag, they might lean on hot sauce or salsa for that tangy burst instead. There’s no right or wrong way to make the best nachos, it just comes down to using the best ingredients. Here at Sporked, we’ve taste tested everything from tortilla chips to black olives to the best cheese for nachos (sauce or shreds—up to you) so you can make these best homemade nachos this side of the Rio Grande.
