Kimberly Stewart Wiki Biography

Kimberly Stewart was born on the 21st August 1979 in Holmby Hills, Los Angeles, California, USA. She is perhaps best known as rock icon Rod Stewart’s daughter, but is also an actress, model, reality TV star, fashion designer, and socialite.

Have you ever wondered how rich Kimberly Stewart is, as of mid-2016? According to authoritative sources, Stewart’s estimated net worth is $10 million. Modeling was her main source of income, but acting and fashion designing have also added a significant chunk of money to her bank account.

Kimberly Alana Stewart was born to Rod and Alana Stewart, the second of eight of Rod’s children. Kimberly attended Buckley School in Sherman Oaks, California, and matriculated in 1994. Always interested in acting and modeling, Kimberly spent several years with acting coaches Ivana Chubbuck and Janet Alhanti, after which she studied in London with personal theater coaches Penny Dyer and Barbara Houseman. Also, Kimberly learned singing from Mark Meylan.

Kimberly Stewart has appeared on many magazine covers, the most notable being “Tatler” and “Vanity Fair”. Bryan Adams, the famous Canadian rocker, was one photographer. Her modeling career started when she was 19, and she realized that could earn a lot of money by posing for some worldwide fashion companies; Tommy Hilfiger, American Vogue, Elle, Italian Vogue, L’uomo Vogue, and Maxmara are among them. One contract with underwear line Ultimo earned her $120,000.

Also when she was just 19, Kimberly launched her own fashion collection named “Pinky Starfish”. Stewart used French vintage fabrics on the dresses which were sold in Barney’s, Tracey Ross, and Lisa Kline, and started her net worth rising.
Her acting career begins in 2001 with a role in the television series called “Going to California”, written by Scott Rosenberg and directed by Ted Demme. In the year after, she appeared in “Undeclared”, by Judd Apatow. Kimberly’s debut movie was “Black and White”, directed by James Toback, starring Robert Downey Jr. and Jared Leto. In 2011, Kimberly starred in Sean Hackett’s movie named “Homecoming”. These few roles have helped her to increase the net worth.
