Shop Priscilla Presleys Most Iconic Looks 2023

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Move aside Austin Butler and Jacob Elordi — it’s Priscilla Presley’s turn to bask in the spotlight. The ex-wife of the late Elvis is played by Cailee Spaeny in the new movie Priscilla, which just dropped in theaters. Priscilla shows a side of the relationship audiences have never seen before: A young girl under the influence of an incredibly famous and powerful man. She met the King of Rock and Roll as a mere teenager before he was thrust into stardom with very public — and not so public — triumphs and tribulations (as showcased in Butler’s movie, Elvis, released back in 2022).

Since then, she’s become an icon, businesswoman, and fashion inspiration. And with that fashion inspiration, Priscilla serves some serious looks in the new film. We put together outfits inspired by the movie’s most notable scenes so that you can recreate them for yourself. It’s the subtle details that really take her fashion to the next level.
