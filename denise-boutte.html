Denise Boutte best known for her roles in Nola, Victoria Campbell in Secrets, and Trina in Why Did I Get Married? She is well renowned for her work as a producer and actress in the TV show Bronx SIU.

Her lucrative acting and modeling career is her main source of money. According to reports, an actress’s average annual wage is roughly $55,271. Due to her expertise and acting abilities, her income must be more than the average.

Born in the state of Louisiana

Denise Boutte was born in Maurice, Louisiana, United States of America, on January 19, 1982. Boutte’s nationality is American, and she is of African-American origin.

Nelson Joseph is her father, while Betty is her mother. Her father has already passed away. She was raised in a rural area near Lafayette.

North Vermillion High School was her alma mater. She went to Louisiana State University to get her MCOM after graduating from high school. While at campus, she worked in a Dallas advertising business. In 1999, she finished her schooling.

Kevin Boutte is her husband

Denise is a mother of one and a married woman. Kevin Boutte, whom she met at a Lafayette Junior Daughter/Junior Knights Catholic teen talent competition, is her husband. Before walking down the aisle in 2003, they dated for a while.

Denise’s Acting and Producing Career

Denise Boutte made her television debut in 2004 on the show Caliente. She appeared in films like Death Valley: The Revenge of Bloody Bill the same year. Boutte then went on to make cameo appearances in a number of television shows and films. She was cast as a supporting part in the soap opera Days of Our Lives, in which she appeared in seven episodes.

In 2006, she earned the lead part of Nola in the film Nola. She then went on to star in films such as Why Did I Get Married?, Extreme Movie Welcome to Los Feliz, and N-Secure. She landed the lead role of Sasha Brown in the television series Meet the Browns, alongside Logan Browning, Tyler Perry, Lamman Rucker, and Markice Moore.
